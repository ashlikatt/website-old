My Games

Here is a collection of my main gamedev projects.


Untitled Sewer Game

This was a Game Jam project developed on-and-off over two weeks. I completed the project alone, and updated the game afterwards to polish and expand on various mechanics.
It may be played here, however is still heavily unfinished.


Parkour

This is a game that I made when I was around 14 or so. It's a simple parkour game that generates its levels.
It may be played here.


Card Games

When I was bored in Computer Science class my senior year of high school, I made a very simple card game engine. I implemented Freecell, which may be played here, and Black Jack which may be played here.


Twenty Four

Twenty Four is a concept for a puzzle/exploration game designed around being stuck in Time Limbo. It has heavy influence from Yume Nikki and its successor, Yume 2kki.

Several tools have been developed for potential future development of the game, including my Map Planner.
